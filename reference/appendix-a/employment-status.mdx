## Overview

The `EMPLOYMENT_STATUS` field captures the client's employment situation at admission and discharge. This is an important outcome measure and social determinant of health.

## Employment Categories

### Employed (01-02, 14-16)
Engaged in productive work:
- **Full-Time** - 35+ hours per week
- **Part-Time** - Less than 35 hours per week
- **Supported** - Employment with support services
- **Sheltered** - Protected work environment
- **Volunteer** - Unpaid productive activity

### Unemployed (03-05)
Not currently working:
- **Looking** - Actively seeking employment
- **Not Looking** - Not seeking (discouraged, other reasons)
- **Disabled** - Unable to work due to disability

### Not in Labor Force (06-09)
Not seeking employment:
- **Retired** - Left workforce due to age
- **Student** - Enrolled in education
- **Homemaker** - Managing household

### Military (10-11)
Active military service:
- **Active Duty** - Full-time military
- **Reserves** - Part-time military service

### Institutional (12-13)
Currently in an institution:
- **Incarcerated** - In jail or prison
- **Residential** - In treatment facility

## Employment as Outcome

Compare `EMPLOYMENT_STATUS` between admission and discharge to measure employment outcomes:

```csv
# Admission - Unemployed
RECORD_TYPE,EMPLOYMENT_STATUS
ADM,03

# Discharge - Part-time employed
RECORD_TYPE,EMPLOYMENT_STATUS
DIS,02
```

### Positive Outcome Categories

Transitions to any of these codes typically represent improved functioning:
- `01` Full-Time
- `02` Part-Time
- `06` Retired (age-appropriate)
- `07`, `08` Student
- `14` Supported Employment

<Tip>
For clients with disabilities, `14` (Supported Employment) or `15` (Sheltered Workshop) may represent excellent outcomes.
</Tip>

## Age-Appropriate Considerations

| Age Group | Expected Codes |
|-----------|---------------|
| Under 16 | 07, 08 (Student), 99 (Other) |
| 16-18 | 01, 02, 07, 08 |
| 18-65 | 01-05, 07-09, 14-16 |
| 65+ | 01, 02, 06 (Retired) |

## Cross-Validation

| If `EMPLOYMENT_STATUS` | Then Check |
|------------------------|------------|
| `07` or `08` (Student) | Age is school-appropriate |
| `06` (Retired) | Age typically 55+ |
| `10` or `11` (Military) | `VETERAN_STATUS` = `0` (active) |
| `12` (Incarcerated) | `LEGAL_STATUS` = `11` or `12` |
