## Overview

The `LIVING_SITUATION` field captures the client's housing status at admission and discharge. This information is critical for understanding social determinants of health and tracking housing outcomes.

## Living Situation Codes

| Code | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `01` | Independent - Own/Rent |
| `02` | Living with Family |
| `03` | Living with Friends |
| `04` | Sober Living/Recovery Housing |
| `05` | Homeless - Shelter |
| `06` | Homeless - Street/Unsheltered |
| `07` | Homeless - Transitional Housing |
| `08` | Residential Treatment Facility |
| `09` | Halfway House |
| `10` | Nursing Home/Long-Term Care |

## Usage Example

```csv
LIVING_SITUATION
01
```

## Living Situation Categories

### Stable Housing (01-03)
Permanent housing arrangements:
- **Independent** - Client maintains own residence
- **Family** - Living with family members
- **Friends** - Living with friends

### Recovery Housing (04, 09)
Structured living environments supporting recovery:
- **Sober Living** - Drug/alcohol-free housing
- **Halfway House** - Transitional recovery residence

### Homeless/Unstable (05-07, 16-17)
Housing instability or homelessness:
- **Shelter** - Emergency or temporary shelter
- **Unsheltered** - Streets, cars, encampments
- **Transitional** - Time-limited housing program
- **Hotel/Motel** - Temporary paid accommodation
- **Doubled Up** - Staying with others due to hardship

### Institutional (08, 10-15)
Residential facilities or institutions:
- **Residential Treatment** - Current treatment facility
- **Nursing Home** - Long-term care
- **Jail/Prison** - Incarcerated
- **Hospital** - Currently hospitalized
- **Foster Care** - State custody (youth)
- **Group Home** - Youth residential setting
- **Assisted Living** - Supportive housing for elderly/disabled

## Tracking Housing Changes

Compare `LIVING_SITUATION` between admission and discharge to measure housing outcomes:

```csv
# Admission - Homeless at shelter
RECORD_TYPE,LIVING_SITUATION
ADM,05

# Discharge - Independent housing
RECORD_TYPE,LIVING_SITUATION
DIS,01
```

<Note>
Housing stability is a key outcome measure. Track transitions from unstable (05-07, 16-17) to stable (01-04) housing.
</Note>

## Special Populations

### Youth
For clients under 18, consider:
- `13` (Foster Care)
- `14` (Group Home)
- `02` (Living with Family)

### Older Adults
For elderly clients, consider:
- `10` (Nursing Home)
- `15` (Assisted Living)
- `01` (Independent with supports)
