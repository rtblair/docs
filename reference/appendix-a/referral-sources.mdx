## Overview

The `REFERRAL_SOURCE` field identifies how the client came to receive services. This helps track pathways into treatment and informs service planning.

## Referral Categories

### Self/Informal (01-02)
Client initiates treatment without formal referral.

### Healthcare (03-09)
Referral from medical or behavioral health providers.

### Educational (10)
Referral from schools, colleges, or educational programs.

### Employment (11)
Referral through workplace or employee assistance programs.

### Criminal Justice (12-18)
Court-ordered or criminal justice system referrals.

### Insurance/Managed Care (19)
Referral through health plan or care manager.

### Community (20-25)
Referral from community organizations and services.

## Criminal Justice Referrals

<Note>
When `REFERRAL_SOURCE` is a criminal justice code (12-18), the `CRIMINAL_JUSTICE_REFERRAL` field should be set to `1`.
</Note>

### Court-Ordered Treatment

For court-ordered clients:
1. Set appropriate referral source (12-16)
2. Set `CRIMINAL_JUSTICE_REFERRAL` = `1`
3. Include `COURT_CASE_NUMBER` if applicable
4. Set appropriate `LEGAL_STATUS`
