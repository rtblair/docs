## Overview

Service identifiers classify the type of behavioral health service being provided. The `SERVICE_IDENTIFIER` field is required on ADMISSION records to identify the primary treatment modality.

## Service Category Descriptions

### Outpatient Services (001-005)
Non-residential treatment where clients attend scheduled appointments.

### Residential Services (010-013)
24-hour care in a treatment facility with varying intensity levels.

### Inpatient Services (020-021)
Hospital-based treatment for acute conditions.

### Support Services (030-033)
Wraparound services supporting recovery.

### MAT Services (040-042)
Medication-assisted treatment for substance use disorders.

### Crisis Services (050-052)
Immediate intervention for behavioral health emergencies.

### Intensive Services (060)
High-intensity community-based treatment.

### Specialized Services (061-062)
Services for specific populations or conditions.

### Youth Services (070-072)
Services specifically designed for youth (under 18).

## Usage Example

```csv
SERVICE_IDENTIFIER
001
```

## Service Level Transitions

When a client transitions between service levels:

1. Discharge from current service
2. Create new admission with new service identifier
3. Link via `BHASO_CLIENT_IDENTIFIER`

<Note>
A client may have multiple active admissions for different service types (e.g., outpatient therapy + MAT).
</Note>
